Adopt An Animal

Here at Naturetrust, we aim to create a collaborative relationship between gaming companies and charitable organisations in order to help our world, the ecosystem and the wildlife that inhabits it. When you purchase an adoption pack we will contribute a whopping 70% to the associated charity.


You may have noticed that we don’t offer any big cat adoption packs. Our adoption packs may actually seem a little strange. That’s because we focus on charities that go unnoticed by so many, despite how their work is incredibly important for sustaining the environment. At Naturetrust, we trust in nature, and understand what animals and organisms are essential for keeping our planet alive and healthy. That’s why we offer adoption packs for bees, sharks, and even plankton, because we recognise that losing them could be detrimental for everyone living on Earth.

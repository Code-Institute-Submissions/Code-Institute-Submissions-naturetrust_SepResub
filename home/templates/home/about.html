About Us

Here at Naturetrust, we aim to create a collaborative relationship between gaming companies and charitable organisations in order to help our world, the ecosystem and the wildlife that inhabits it.

But wildlife and environmental charities are everywhere, so what makes us so unique? For starters, we don’t ask you to blindly donate. Instead we ask you to use our platform to buy your games. For each game you purchase with us, we will contribute 12% of its total price to one of our partnered charities. We even offer adoption packs that you can purchase, and for them we contribute a whopping 70% to the associated charity.

But there’s something else that makes us stand out from the crowd! You may have noticed that we don’t offer any big cat adoption packs. Our adoption packs may actually seem a little strange. That’s because we focus on charities that go unnoticed by so many, despite how their work is incredibly important for sustaining the environment. At Naturetrust, we trust in nature, and understand what animals and organisms are essential for keeping our planet alive and healthy. That’s why we offer adoption packs for bees, sharks, and even plankton, because we recognise that losing them could be detrimental for everyone living on Earth.

Even if you do not wish to buy from us today, please help us spread awareness to our cause, by sharing our website and its information with family and friends, and help us make a contribution which could help our planet thrive again!
